Natural Home Remedies

Safe and Simple Cures for Common Ailments

1. Cold & Cough

  • Drink warm water with honey and lemon.
  • Inhale steam with a few drops of eucalyptus oil.
  • Ginger tea with tulsi (holy basil) helps soothe the throat.

2. Headache

  • Apply peppermint oil to temples for relief.
  • Drink ginger tea to reduce inflammation.
  • Massage with lavender essential oil for relaxation.

3. Indigestion

  • Chew fennel seeds after meals.
  • Drink warm water with lemon and baking soda.
  • Have a cup of peppermint tea.

4. Acne

  • Apply aloe vera gel directly on the skin.
  • Use a paste of turmeric and honey.
  • Wash your face twice a day with a gentle cleanser.

5. Sore Throat

  • Gargle with warm salt water.
  • Drink turmeric milk before sleeping.
  • Have herbal teas like chamomile or licorice root tea.

6. Constipation

  • Drink warm water with lemon in the morning.
  • Eat fiber-rich foods like papaya, prunes, or flax seeds.
  • Use castor oil (1 tsp) with warm milk before bed (occasionally).

7. Nausea

  • Sip ginger tea or chew a small piece of raw ginger.
  • Smell lemon or peppermint essential oil.
  • Drink rice water or clear fluids.

8. Insomnia

  • Drink warm milk with a pinch of nutmeg or turmeric.
  • Avoid screen time an hour before bed.
  • Try lavender essential oil on the pillow.

9. High Blood Pressure (Mild)

  • Consume garlic (raw or boiled).
  • Drink beetroot juice regularly.
  • Practice deep breathing or meditation.

10. Joint Pain / Arthritis

  • Apply a warm turmeric paste to painful joints.
  • Drink turmeric milk or ginger tea.
  • Soak in a warm Epsom salt bath.

11. Dandruff

  • Massage scalp with coconut oil mixed with lemon juice.
  • Use apple cider vinegar rinse after shampooing.
  • Apply aloe vera gel directly to the scalp.

12. Toothache

  • Apply clove oil to the affected area.
  • Rinse mouth with warm salt water.
  • Use garlic paste as a natural antiseptic.

13. Burns (Minor)

  • Apply aloe vera gel to the burn.
  • Place a cool (not ice-cold) compress.
  • Use honey as a natural antibacterial agent.

14. Dark Circles / Puffy Eyes

  • Place chilled cucumber slices over the eyes.
  • Use cold tea bags (green or chamomile).
  • Apply almond oil under the eyes before bed.

15. Menstrual Cramps

  • Drink chamomile or ginger tea.
  • Use a hot water bag on the abdomen.
  • Try light stretching or yoga poses.
